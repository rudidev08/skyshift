Skyshift Orientation

Five nations share a cluster of sectors. Each one has what others need, and needs what others have. Enjoy the universe as it evolves.

01 A day in the cluster

A

Trade

Nations trade what they need most, from what they have most. Each route is an answer to supply and demand, drawn between the stations that need it most.

B

Build

Nations build new stations directly, or contract another nation to do it. Traders ferry hulls to the new site — which shows up with its own final-station icon, just emptier than the parent.

C

Emigrate

When a cluster fills up, emigration happens via a Wayfair Generational Ship. Nearby stations transfer their people aboard, and once it departs, space opens up for the cluster to keep growing.
